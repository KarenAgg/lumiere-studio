About the studio

We don't take photos.
We make them.

Lumière is a fine art photography studio founded by Clara Moraes in 2018. We believe that a great photograph is never accidental — it is the result of intention, light, and a deep understanding of the subject.

From intimate portrait sessions to large-scale commercial campaigns, every project starts with a conversation and ends with images that last a lifetime.
